Twilight Struggle: Quickplay Instructions

1. PLACE STARTING INFLUENCE

A good start for the Soviets is East Germany (4), Poland (4) and Yugoslavia (1). A good start for the Americans is West Germany (4) and Italy (3).

2. PICK HEADLINE CARD

The event will happen before the start of the round. The highest card number goes first, with the US event happening first in case of ties.

3. PLAY ROUNDS:

The USSR and US take turns picking cards and playing them. All cards may be played as follows:

  1. Play Event:

    The event on the card happens.

  2. Play Operation Points (OPS)

    You can use the points on the card as follows:

    1. Place Influence

      Add influence to a country in which you have influence or any country adjacent to it.
    2. Coup

      You can only coup a country in which your opponent has influence. As DEFCON lowers certain regions of the board become off-limits for coups. Couping in a Battleground Country lowers DEFCON by one point. The "power" of the coup is the total points on the card you play added to a roll of the dice minus twice the stability of the country (the number in the top-right of each country box).
    3. Realignment

      Roll the dice and remove influence from one player. Controlling surrounding countries and having more influence than your opponent in the target country increases your chance of winning.

  3. Space Race:

    You may discard one card per turn on the Space Race, OPS permitting. If you are lucky, you will advance one in the Space Race track.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
  • If you play a card belonging to your opponent the event WILL happen regardless. You can choose whether it happens before or after you play your operations points.
  • If you have a Scoring Card, you MUST play it during your turn.
  • Discard cards you do not wish to play on the Space Race
